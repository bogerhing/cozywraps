Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?

Standard UK delivery is 2–4 business days. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 days.

What is your returns policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! All orders come beautifully packaged by default. Add a gift note at checkout and we'll include a handwritten card.

Can I place a custom order?

Absolutely. We take on bespoke commissions for weddings, corporate gifts, and interior projects. Reach out to discuss.
