Our Story

About Cozy Wraps

Born from a belief that home should feel like a warm embrace — every single day.

How It Started

A small workshop. A big belief.

Cozy Wraps began in 2014 in a small Edinburgh workshop owned by textile artist Clara Hargreaves. Armed with two looms, a passion for natural fibers, and a deep dissatisfaction with mass-produced homeware, Clara set out to create blankets worthy of being passed down through generations.

What started as a one-woman operation quickly grew through word of mouth. People didn't just love the blankets — they told their friends, gifted them at weddings, and wrote letters (actual letters) to describe the comfort they'd brought into their homes.

Today Cozy Wraps ships to over 30 countries and works with a small team of artisans across Scotland, Peru, and Portugal. But the values haven't changed one bit.

Honesty in materials

We name our fibers, trace their origins, and never use synthetic blends disguised as natural.

Fair pay, always

Every artisan we work with earns a living wage. We publish our pay rates openly.

Slow design

We release new pieces only when they're ready. No seasonal pressure, no trend chasing.

Planet-first packaging

100% recyclable packaging, minimal materials, and zero plastic in every shipment.

Sustainability

Caring for the planet we all share

We believe that beautiful things shouldn't cost the earth — literally. Our sustainability program covers every part of the supply chain, from fiber sourcing to your front door.

We are a certified B Corp, use 100% renewable energy in our workshop, and plant one tree for every order placed.
